Who I am

Hi There!

I am an experienced digital media and production professional currently working as a Product Developer for AND Digital, a web consultancy agency based in London. I also run Jupiter and the Giraffe where I also build products and websites for clients owning the end-to-end process.

Before my work at Digital Detox, for three years I leveraged and honed my technical expertise using Adobe CS and Developer skills (HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, MySQL, Actionscript) on key projects at Framestore in their Design and Digital portfolio. A rewarding experience spanning interactive, VFX, motion graphics, titles and credits, and motion capture. Highlights include building British Airways’ “Take a Plane Down Your Street” web/Facebook app, which has been said to be The most successful social media app ever (Mail On Sunday, 23 June 2012), titles and end rollers for the film “Tyrannosaur”, and working on visual graphics for Secret Cinema’s “Prometheus” show.

In addition, my interest in live performance led me to co-create Velvet Trumpet, a theatre company in which I am Production Manager. I was thrilled to be at the helm of their first production “A Model Life”, which ran at The Network Theatre to rave reviews. Velvet Trumpet's latest production 'Don't Disturb the Driver' was featured at the 2013 Edinburgh Fringe, and we now hope to hold regular nights at the Hen & Chickens Theatre Pub with our first show being "Toast".

Specialties: Front end web Development (HTML/CSS/Javascript), VFX (After Effects), Project Management/Production.
